# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, constructive, and professional.

Examples of expected behavior:

- give actionable technical feedback
- assume good intent and ask clarifying questions
- keep discussions focused on improving the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or behavior
- doxxing or sharing private information
- bad-faith disruption of discussions or reviews

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, reject contributions, or block participants for violations.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact maintainers privately through GitHub or security/support channels.
